About Us


We are a family farm by all definitions. We started our farm in February 2015,in our backyard, with 8 chicken. It did not take us long to really love having farm animals. Which lead us to adding more chickens and a goat. At that time we decided we needed more space to be able to farm the way we wanted too. In May of 2015 we moved our chickens and goats to a 2 acres green belt on the boarder of Lehi and Saratoga Springs, Utah. And then we added a horse, cows, pig, and more goats.
