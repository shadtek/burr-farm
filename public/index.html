About Us


We are a family farm by all definitions. We started our farm in February 2015, in our backyard, with 8 chickens. It didn't take us long to really love having farm animals. Which lead us to adding more chickens and a goat. At that time we decided we needed more space so that we could farm the way we wanted to. In May of 2015 we moved our chickens and goats to a 2 acre green belt on the border of Lehi and Saratoga Springs, Utah. Then we added a horse, cows, pig, and more goats.

The property needed animal enclosures and houses built so we decided we would do it as a family. We started using the farm as an educational experience for our 6 children who are home schooled. The kids learned very quickly how to use their math skills in a very practical way. There is so much character in every little house out there that I fall in love with it more each day. Our children have also started to enjoy sharing what they have learned while building the farm. We started inviting people and school groups out to get hands on with farming. We found that families really enjoy coming out and experiencing farming together and our young guests really love learning from our children.

We are still working on improving our farm every day. Some of our current projects are installing a water well, expanding the goat yard and designing and building an irrigation system for the pasture. These projects would not be possible without the great knowledge Grandpa Burr has about farming. Being able to work on these projects have really drawn our family and our extended family closer together. And it has made our children very hard workers too. We are striving to create an educational experience for our local community, so others may be inspired to try their hands at farming. It is a wholesome activity that can create a life long change in how we view our food. Which will affect our community by creating new farms and more families working towards a self sustaining lifestyle. Farming can be difficult, but it is so worth it.

So we invite all who would like to learn more to come on out to the farm. You can try your hand at farming and see if it is a fit for you. We love making new friends and look forward to having you as a friend.
